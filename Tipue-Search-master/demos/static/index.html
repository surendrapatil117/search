Search Help

You can add:

  • A question mark? wildcard anywhere in a word to replace any single character.
    Example: ?ead will match Read and Head.
  • An asterisk* wildcard at the beginning or end of a word to replace zero or more characters.
    Example: Plugin* will match both Plugin and Plugins.
  • "Quotation marks" around a groups of words to search for the group of words.
    Example: "search engine".
  • A -dash before a word to exclude all results that include that word. For groups of words, put the "-dash inside the left quotation mark" to exclude the group of words.
    Example: -plugin or "-jQuery plugin" will exclude search results containing plugin or "jQuery plugin"

All searches are case insensitive however characters with accents are considered different than those without. Example: Searching for the word resume will fail to find text containing resum√©.
